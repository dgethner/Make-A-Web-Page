Project Management

Picutre was taken from youracclaim website

After college, I decided to continue my career and personal development by perusing a project management certification. I signed up for a 5-Saturday course totaling 40 hours of instruction to obtain the knowledge needed to sit for the certification. After taking a few days off work to prepare for the exam, I sat for the 150 questions / 3-hour long exam. Once the exam was over, I received my score and learned that I had achieved my goal! I became a Certified Associate Project Manager. I have held this title since September 2016. I plan on sitting for my PMP (Project Management Professional) certification once I fulfill all the requirements to sit for the exam.
